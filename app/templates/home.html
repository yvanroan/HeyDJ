How It Works

1. DJ/Host Setup

Scan the QR code/link at the venue to join the event

2. Connect Live

Users submit song requests by entering the artist and title. DJs can review requests in real time.

3. End Event

Once the party is over, the DJ ends the event. Clearing all pending requests and the event is over.

4. Stay in the Loop

Get personalized notifications about events involving your DJ and/or the venue
